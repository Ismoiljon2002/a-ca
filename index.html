About ACA

Alarming System

If ACA cannot detect the driver's eyes for 5 seconds, it sounds loudly as an alarm to warn the driver. Unless it detects the eyes again, volume of its sound increases gradually.

Working Procedure

The camera scans the driver's face, open eyes, mouth, and condition of eyelids. It gathers data counting number of yawnings and eye blinks

Automatic Breaking System

For automated mobility Driver assistance systems decelerate the car and turn on left-right turning indicator lights. System helps to prevent accidents which are caused by fatigue and microsleep.
